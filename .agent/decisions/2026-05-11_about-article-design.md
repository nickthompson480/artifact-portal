## Choice

"About the Artifact Portal" article is styled as a dark editorial document using the portal's exact color tokens (`#0C0E14` background, `#E8A042` amber accent, etc.) with a different but complementary font stack: Cormorant Garamond (display), Lora (body), Geist Mono (labels/code).

## Alternatives considered

- Warm parchment / editorial (same as the existing Fraunces/Newsreader article) — rejected to avoid repetition
- Neutral/technical style — rejected as too dry for a founder's-note piece

## Rationale

The "meta" concept: an article about the portal that looks like the portal itself. When opened in the dark-themed viewer, the document mirrors its container — same darkness, same amber, same typographic hierarchy. Intentionally self-referential. Font stack is different from both the portal UI (DM Serif/DM Sans/DM Mono) and the existing article (Fraunces/Newsreader/JetBrains Mono) to give the piece its own voice within the same aesthetic world.
